About Us

At Cherry Delights, we believe in crafting the most delightful pastries to brighten your day. Our recipes are made from the finest ingredients and a pinch of love.

Behind Cherry Delights is a passionate and hardworking student currently in her third year of the ICM program. Her love for baking and cooking started as a childhood hobby, which has since blossomed into a heartfelt pursuit of bringing joy through homemade treats. She balances her academic life with her baking endeavors, creating recipes that are not only delicious but also made with a deep sense of care and dedication. Her dream is to share her creations with as many people as possible, spreading smiles one pastry at a time.
